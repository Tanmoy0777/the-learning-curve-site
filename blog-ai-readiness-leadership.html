Leadership

AI readiness starts with leaders

Why executive enablement is the fastest path to AI adoption.

The 2024 Work Trend Index notes that 66% of leaders would not hire someone without AI skills, yet only 39% of AI users have received company training and just 25% of companies expect to offer AI training this year. The gap between expectations and enablement is now an enterprise risk.

Leadership actions that close the gap

  • Run executive AI immersion sessions tied to key business KPIs.
  • Launch role-based AI training for managers and team leads.
  • Make AI skills progression a visible career pathway.

Leaders who model AI adoption create momentum that cascades across the organization.

Sources: Microsoft Work Trend Index 2024
