The skills triad: AI, security, and tech literacy

Why these three areas are rising together in enterprise learning plans.

The Future of Jobs 2025 digest lists AI and big data, networks and cybersecurity, and technological literacy as the top three fastest-growing skills. Together they form a triad that should anchor modern learning roadmaps.

How to build the triad

  • AI literacy for every function, from sales to operations.
  • Security-first thinking embedded in every AI and cloud track.
  • Technology literacy fundamentals for non-technical teams.

Pairing the triad with role-based labs creates measurable adoption momentum.

Sources: WEF Future of Jobs 2025 (digest)
