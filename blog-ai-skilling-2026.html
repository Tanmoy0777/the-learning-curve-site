AI skilling priorities for 2026

Published for CXOs and enterprise learning leaders.

WEF research shows that 39% of workers’ core skills are expected to change by 2030, with AI and big data, networks and cybersecurity, and technological literacy leading the fastest-growing skills. For 2026, enterprise learning teams need to blend AI fluency with security and data readiness.

What leaders should focus on now

  • Build AI literacy across business roles, not only technical teams.
  • Pair AI and data training with security and governance fundamentals.
  • Create continuous learning loops instead of one-off workshops.

Programs that tie AI foundations to real workflows (sales enablement, customer success, marketing ops) deliver the fastest business ROI.

Sources: WEF Future of Jobs Report 2025 (digest)
