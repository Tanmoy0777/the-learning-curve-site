Cloud certifications roadmap

Sequencing foundational to professional certifications across major vendors.

Across Google Cloud, AWS, and Microsoft, certification paths follow a similar progression: start with foundational or fundamentals, move through associate-level role certifications, and finish with professional or expert tracks. This structure makes it easier to build a unified cloud enablement roadmap for enterprise teams.

Suggested enterprise roadmap

  • Foundational: Cloud fundamentals + security basics.
  • Associate: Role-based engineering, admin, or developer certifications.
  • Professional/Expert: Architecture, DevOps, security, or data specialization.

Pair certifications with applied labs to ensure teams can execute real migrations and optimizations.
