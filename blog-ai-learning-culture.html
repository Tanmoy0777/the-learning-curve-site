AI adoption

Building the AI learning culture

Designing enterprise learning that keeps pace with AI adoption.

LinkedIn’s 2024 AI Learning Day research shows that 80% of employees want to learn more about AI, while only 38% of U.S. executives are helping employees become AI-literate. Closing this gap requires consistent, visible learning moments—not just one-time workshops.

Culture-building tactics

  • Host quarterly AI Learning Days with cross-functional tracks.
  • Curate role-based learning playlists for each business function.
  • Measure adoption by use-case, not just course completion.

Culture shifts happen when teams see AI training tied to real performance metrics.

Sources: LinkedIn AI Learning Day
