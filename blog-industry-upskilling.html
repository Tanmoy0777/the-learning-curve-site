Industry upskilling priorities

How to align learning with the fastest-growing roles in 2025-2026.

WEF highlights that technology roles such as AI and machine learning specialists, big data specialists, and cybersecurity professionals are among the fastest-growing roles globally. Industry-specific upskilling programs need to blend these technical skills with domain expertise.

Industry playbook

  • Healthcare: AI copilots + data governance + security compliance.
  • Financial services: cloud modernization + risk + AI ops.
  • Manufacturing: analytics + automation + IoT security.

Combine vendor certifications with domain labs to accelerate time-to-impact.
